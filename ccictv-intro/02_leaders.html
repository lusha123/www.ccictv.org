傳道─薛忠勇

(408) 437-1263


薛弟兄於1972年隨同家人移民美國加州。初中時被聖詩吸引開始到基督徒會堂參加聚會，1975年受洗歸主。高中畢業後就讀於聖荷西州立大學生物系，曾在一所公立高中任教七年，並經常利用暑假參與各地短宣工作，最後蒙召獻身傳道。1994年在南加州的富樂神學院完成道學碩士。1995年回到母會北區會堂任全職傳道，負責崇拜、詩班、師資栽培、查經訓練等服事。2009年帶領一群同工到三谷植堂。閒時他喜歡遠足、烹飪、電影及旅遊。

Balex’s family immigrated from Hong Kong to California in 1972. He graduated from San Jose State University with a B.A. degree in Biology, and obtained his teaching credential in 1984. After teaching in a high school for 7 years, he followed God’s calling and went to Fuller Theological Seminary in Pasadena for his Master of Divinity degree in 1991. He returned to North Valley CCIC in 1995 and since then has served as full-time minister in the Mandarin congregation in charge of worship, choir, and bible training. In September 2009 he led a group of coworkers to plant a church in Tri Valley.  At leisure he enjoys hiking, cooking, movies and travel.

He attended University of Hawaii, studying International Business, for his under graduate studies, and worked in restaurant management for 9 years.  After receiving his calling to serve Youth, in 2002, he went to Golden Gate Baptist Theological Seminary where he received a MDIV and a MAEL degrees (M.A. in Education Leadership).  He has been serving the youth Ministry over the past 16 years.  He was married in 2011 at the nice young age of 39. He is married to Connie and has 2 children.  They are his son, Keoni and they’re daughter is Kaila. On his spare time, he likes to go fishing, hiking, camping, snowboarding, and BBQing.

He and Meiyin also devoted in children and AWANA ministry.
