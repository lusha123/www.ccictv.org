Programs

Sunday Service

The Youth Ministry of CCIC-TV cultivates a safe and welcoming community for youth that embodies the CCIC-TV Mission. We want our youth to encounter Jesus and be equipped to develop their own authentic faith in Jesus, to establish meaningful connections, and to engage in mission of God.

Each Sunday, at 9:30 am – 10:45 am the youth worship consists of youth led praise (contemporary) followed by Biblical preaching on the pulpit.

Bible study is offered at 11:15 am that follows a book by book, chapter by chapter, verse by verse approach to engage youth in life application. The youth are organized by small group, arranged by appropriate age and gender classes. The youth group consist of 7th through 12th grades.

As we lead our youth to God we partake in Holy Communion once a month on the first Sunday as a means of remembrance. We partake of the cup and the bread to recognize what Jesus has done for our salvation.
