About Us

IMPACT MINISTRY


VISION: We want to empower every one of our youth to know God and have a continual ongoing personal relationship with Him through Jesus here at CCIC-TV. By holistically building up Chinese Christian Families in Tri-Valley with the Word of God and to pass down the love and teachings of Jesus through generations.

PURPOSE: We exist to lead our youth to God, each other, and communities through Christ-centered love and teaching in coordination with CCIC-TV church leadership.

CORE VALUES:

  • YOUTH The passion, vision, and potential of teenagers is not just the future of the Church – it is a vibrant part of the Church today. In the process of inviting students to love and be loved by Christ, we will laugh, play, serve, worship, and engage alongside them. Recognizing the incredible value of teenagers vital for God’s work today.
  • COMMUNITY Our passions are in the places we are in. Communities are our partners and our contexts for ministry. We value the privilege of entering, working alongside and learning from these places filled with history, strengths and struggles. We are continually pursuing ways to better listen to, respond to and love communities.
  • SERVICE It is our honor to serve people that God calls “sons” and “daughters.” We strive to listen closely, speak carefully, serve humbly and participate gratefully in what God is already doing in individuals. In our efforts to meet the unique needs of each person and place, we desire to follow Jesus’ example of servant-leadership.
  • RELATIONSHIP We prioritize our youth. It’s why we pursue intentional interactions, restored relationships and ongoing friendships – because meaningful service stems from seeking each other out. Just as Jesus’ ministry underscores the importance of presence, we desire that programs, projects and plans would never trump people. Rather, we will continue to create experiences that encourage authentic connections with others and with Christ.
  • DEVELOPMENT We have seen God’s life-changing work in people’s stories and recognize our invitation to participate. That is why we seek to grow alongside others in deepening understanding, determining direction, restoring relationships and strengthening faith. As we encounter all kinds of youth, we will continue to work together, challenge our comfort zones, see potential, be inspired and eagerly ask, “What’s next?!”
  • DISCIPLESHIP We as leaders who regularly speak into the lives of young people in their most impressionable years, our attention to this command at must be even greater! We at CCIC-TV Impact drive discipleship by mentoring and sharing our lives with our youth.
